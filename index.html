My Story

My name is Gergely Szabo and I am a web developer. I was born and raised in a beautiful Hungary and currently live in Vienna, Austria.

I studied history and information science, but soon after graduation I moved to London and I lived there for 5 years, working in hospitality and in logistics. This decision led me into some new opportunities and helped me to meet many great people and enjoy new experiences. I was always interested in programming, but since I moved to Vienna, I finished a 6 month intensive web development course and I work every day to get better and improve my technical skills.
